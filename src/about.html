This website was created as a school project. It's only purpose is to provide me a full score and give you a short but fun experience.

How to play?

The game area is divided into two parts. Your main goal is to complete the puzzle on the left side of the screen. However, as you probably noticed, there are no puzzle pieces... To get one, you need to win a game of rock-paper-scissors against the computer! The game area for rock-paper-scissors is found on the right side of your screen.
